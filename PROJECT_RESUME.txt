- Settings, Search, About ‚Üí Additional app features

CAMERA WORKFLOW:
---------------
Step 1: Take Front Photo
        - Camera opens with overlay instructions
        - Auto-crops to center 80% of frame
        - Saves high-quality JPEG

Step 2: Take Back Photo  
        - Camera opens for back of carded car
        - Auto-crops to center 80% of frame
        - ML Kit automatically detects barcode from back photo
        - Extracts barcode string for database matching

Step 3: Enter Car Details
        - Shows detected barcode (if found)
        - User enters: Car Name/Model, Year, Color
        - Simple form validation

Step 4: Save Complete
        - Saves to personal collection in chosen folder
        - Simultaneously uploads to global database
        - Returns to collection view
